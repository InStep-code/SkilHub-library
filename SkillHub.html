About SkilHub Library

SkilHub is a student-built library website designed to make reading accessible and fun for everyone. Our mission is to connect our school community and the public with great books!

Borrowing Rules & FAQ

How long can I borrow a book? Books can be borrowed for 2 weeks.

Do students get discounts? Yes, students with a valid ID get 10% off purchases!

Can I renew my borrowing? Yes, contact us to extend your borrowing period.
